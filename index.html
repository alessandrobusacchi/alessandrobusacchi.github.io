I am an M.Sc. student in Game and Media Technology at Utrecht University, working at the intersection of Computer Vision, Computer Graphics, and Artificial Intelligence. My research focuses on speech-driven 3D facial animation, digital humans, and deep learning methods for character animation.

I am passionate about visual computing, immersive technologies, and interactive media, with a strong interest in building systems that combine machine learning with real-time graphics and human-centered interaction.

I hold a B.Sc. in Information and Communication Technology with a specialization in game development, and I have professional experience as an HMI developer in industrial automation. Through academic projects and research activities, I have gained experience in designing, implementing, and evaluating complex systems across computer vision, graphics, and machine learning domains.

I am particularly interested in research and development roles in visual computing and AI, aiming to contribute to innovative technologies that enhance how people interact with digital content and immersive environments.
